Student Management & Attendance System

Mini Project using Django Framework

Project Overview

The Student Management & Attendance System is a web-based application developed using the Django framework. This system is designed to manage student records efficiently and track their daily attendance in a digital format. It helps educational institutions reduce manual work, avoid errors, and maintain accurate records.

Project Objectives

• To maintain student details such as name, roll number, department, and contact information.
• To record daily attendance in an easy and structured way.
• To allow editing and deleting of attendance records.
• To generate attendance reports in PDF format.
• To provide a user-friendly interface for administrators and teachers.
